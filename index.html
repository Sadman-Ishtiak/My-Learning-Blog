The Einstein equation is

$$ e = mc^2 $$

and the Pythagorean theorem is

$$ a^2 + b^2 = c^2 $$

Here is a simple integral:

$$ \int_0^1 x^2 \, dx = \frac{1}{3} $$

And a summation:

$$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6} $$
