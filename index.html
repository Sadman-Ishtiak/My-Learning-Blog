Checking equations

The Einstein equation is $e = mc^2$.

The Pythagorean theorem is $a^2 + b^2 = c^2$.

Here is a simple integral: $\int_0^1 x^2 \, dx = \frac{1}{3}$.

And a summation: $$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}$$

Finally, a matrix example: $A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \end{pmatrix}$.

And a fraction: $\frac{a}{b}$.
